SK Haji Mahmud, Chemor

Studied at SK Haji Mahmud, Chemor since 'darjah' 1 until 'darjah' 6. I made a lot of friends during my primary school and loved to eat in the class together with my friends (thank God our teachers never find out). My favourite subject during primary school was definitely 'Pendidikan Jasmani', I used to love sports like 'lompat tinggi' and badminton. But now I just feel too lazy to even hold the badminton racket. I really miss my teachers especially Cikgu Halimatun and Teacher Puteri. Sadly, I got a bad news in 2016, which was during my SPM year that my beloved teacher, Teacher Puteri had passed away due to cancer. May Allah bless her soul. Al-fatihah.

UiTM Machang, Kelantan (Diploma)

I studied at Kelantan in January, 2018- July, 2020. Kelantan is one of my favourite state because of the food. I love their slangs and admire their beautiful faces especially that one who has Thai-mix or looks like Arab people. I've been to Kelantan few times before I got the chance to study there. It was quite awkward first few weeks because I had no friends and I didn't understand what they are saying because of the slangs. Days by days, months by months, I slowly understood and got to learn and speak in Kelantanese slangs. Studied in Kelantan was definitely one of my precious memories and got me some bitter-sweet memories that I will never forget.
